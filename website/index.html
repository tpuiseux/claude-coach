Get up and running in minutes.

Getting Started

Claude Coach works with Claude.ai or Claude Code. Follow these steps to create your first training plan.

  1. Download the skill from the latest release
  2. Add to Claude.ai: Settings → Capabilities → Add skill. Also add *.strava.com to allowed domains
  3. Start a conversation with Claude and ask for a training plan for your target event
  4. Connect Strava when prompted, or describe your fitness level manually
  5. Review your plan in the interactive viewer and export workouts to your devices
# Example prompt for Claude

"Help me create a training plan for the
Boston Marathon on April 21st 2025
using the coach skill.

I've been running for 3 years and my
current weekly mileage is about 30 miles.
My goal is to finish under 4 hours."
